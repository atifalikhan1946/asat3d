ASAT 3D Technologies is a dynamic industry based in Kalaburagi specializing in 3D Printing, Product Designing, 3D Scanning, Photogrammetry, and related services. True to our tagline, "A Hub for Innovators," we are the ultimate destination for professionals, students, enthusiasts, and anyone passionate about advancing innovation in their field. We proudly offer comprehensive training programs, hands-on workshops, and academic internships, equipping individuals with essential knowledge and practical experience in these transformative technologies.

Our Services

ASAT 3D Technologies offers precision 3D printing services specializing in FDM with materials like PLA, ABS, and PETG. For more advanced needs, we collaborate with trusted vendors to provide SLA and SLS options, ensuring high-quality results across various industries, including automotive, healthcare, and consumer products.

ASAT 3D Technologies provides 3D design services for industries like automotive, healthcare, aerospace, architecture, and consumer products. We support manufacturing methods such as sheet metal fabrication, laser cutting, CNC machining, and injection molding, delivering precise, functional digital models optimized for each project’s needs, ready for production.

ASAT 3D Technologies delivers advanced 3D scanning solutions for industries such as construction, medical devices, consumer electronics, heavy machinery, and cultural heritage. Our high-precision scanning captures intricate details, enabling accurate digital models for inspection, reverse engineering, and restoration, ensuring every project’s unique requirements are met with tailored, manufacturable data.

ASAT 3D Technologies offers hands-on workshops for students, industry professionals, enthusiasts, and individuals, covering practical skills in 3D Designing, 3D Scanning, and 3D Printing. Tailored to each audience, our workshops provide a comprehensive learning experience, from foundational concepts to advanced applications, empowering participants to explore and innovate within the world of 3D Technology.

ASAT 3D Technologies offers academic internships, providing students with practical experience in 3D Designing, 3D Scanning, and 3D Printing technologies. Our internships combine hands-on learning with real-world applications, allowing participants to develop essential industry skills and insights while contributing to impactful projects in fields like engineering, healthcare, architecture, and product design.
