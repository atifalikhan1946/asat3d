ASAT 3D Technologies is a pioneering brand offering advanced 3D printing and 3D Design solutions across a broad range of sectors, including medical, aerospace, fashion, robotics, automotive, architecture, construction, prototyping, art and sculpture, food, cosplay, etc.

Our commitment to delivering top-tier products and services aligns with our focus on quality and timely completion. By deeply understanding each client’s specific needs, we strive to exceed expectations with every project. Equipped with in-house 3D printers, we efficiently manage tight timelines while upholding the highest quality standards.

Beyond 3D Printing, ASAT 3D Technologies excels in 3D Designing, 3D Scanning, and Reverse Engineering. We provide specialized support to industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, automotive, architecture, etc helping them achieve precision and innovation in every detail.

In addition to supporting established industries, we are dedicated to empowering students, startups, and small businesses. From academic projects to custom design needs, we offer hands-on guidance and expertise at every stage.

We also host workshops, internships, and training programs for students, industry professionals, and educational institutions, aiming to expand knowledge and proficiency in 3D technologies.

At ASAT 3D Technologies, our goal is to lead on a global scale, setting a high standard in 3D services and innovation. The brand was founded by our visionary CEO, Mr. Asim Ali Khan, whose dedication and expertise have been instrumental in shaping the journey of this organisation. The brand name "ASAT" stands for "Amazing, Superb, and Tremendous," reflecting our drive to surpass limits and redefine possibilities.
